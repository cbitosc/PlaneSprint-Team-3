How to get started with coding?


If you're wondering how to become a coder in three to six months and have no coding, web development or software engineering experience, you'll want to enroll in a coding bootcamp. Coding bootcamps are intensive programs that help students build foundational knowledge in core technologies. You can also practice in platforms like codechef, hackerrank and leet code. You can use books, online courses, YouTube tutorials, and online communities to help you master the fundamentals.
